$\ta = X^T \alpha$

kernel function $k(x_i, x_j) = (x_i)^T (x_j) = \$

here $x_i$ is the ith row of $ X\in R^(N * D) $, ie, it is the ith data point

ie, gram matrix: $K = XX^T$ and $K_{ij} = k(x_i, x_j)$ is the inner product of the i and j th data point(row).

$x \in R^D$
$k(\mathbf{x},\mathbf{x’}) \in R^{F x F}$ F is number of basic,
say if $\phi(x) = x$, then k in size[1, 1], if $\phi(x) = [x x^2]$ then k in size [2, 2]

$k(\mathbf{x},\mathbf{x’}) \in R^{1 x 1}$
eg: $\phi(x) = [cos(x) sin(x) x^2]^T = (cos(x), sin(x), x^2) \in R^3$
$k(\mathbf{x1},\mathbf{x2}) = <\phi(x1), \phi(x2)> = <[cos(x1) sin(x1) x1^2]^T, [cos(x2) sin(x2) x2^2]^T> = \sum_i \phi_i(x1)*\phi_i(x2)$

$K_{ij} = k(\mathbf{x_i},\mathbf{x_j}) = entries \in K$
$x_i : cols \in X$
